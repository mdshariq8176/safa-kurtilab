# Return & Refund Policy

Thank you for shopping at **Safa Kurtilab**. We take great pride in our traditional tailoring, premium fabrics, and design quality. However, if your garment does not meet your expectations or if you require a different size, we are here to assist.

---

### 1. The 7-Day Return Window
* We offer a strict **7-day return window** for our retail catalog garments.
* You must contact us or raise a return request within **7 calendar days** from the date the package is marked as `DELIVERED` by our 3PL courier tracking system.
* Any request submitted after this 7-day period will not be eligible for returns or exchanges.

### 2. Return Eligibility Criteria
To qualify for a return or replacement, the garment must satisfy the following conditions:
* The Kurta, Anarkali, or outfit set must be completely unworn, unwashed, and free of any odor, stains, or alterations.
* All original product tags, luxury brand labels, and packaging elements must be intact and attached to the product.
* B2B custom-tailored orders or bulk wholesale configurations are not eligible for standard retail returns unless they suffer from manufacturing defects.

### 3. Refund Processing Timeline
Once your return parcel arrives back at our Shahpur Jat design studio warehouse and is inspected for quality:
* We will send you an email confirmation approving or rejecting the return request.
* If approved, the refund will be processed immediately.
* According to standard banking rules and payment gateway timelines, the refunded amount will reflect in your original payment method (Bank Account, Credit/Debit Card, or UPI) within **3 to 5 business days (working days)**.
* Shipping charges and custom duty costs are non-refundable.
