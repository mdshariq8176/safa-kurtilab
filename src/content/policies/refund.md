# Return & Refund Policy (B2B Wholesale Model)

Welcome to **Safa Kurtilab** (Maison Safa). As a dedicated B2B wholesale manufacturer and bulk custom tailoring design studio, our operations are strictly governed by commercial wholesale trading standards. By placing a wholesale order on our storefront, you acknowledge and agree to the following return and refund rules:

---

### 1. Strict Wholesale Defect-Only Policy
* **No Buyer Remorse Returns**: We do not accept returns or exchanges for change of mind, sizing preferences, or unsold retail stock.
* **Manufacturing Defects Only**: Returns, exchanges, or credit notes are strictly limited to verified manufacturing defects (e.g., structural fabric tears, severe weaving flaws, incorrect custom sizing deviations from approved specifications).

### 2. Mandatory Cargo-Opening Video Verification
To prevent fraudulent claims and ensure transparent resolutions, all claims for manufacturing defects or shipping damages are subject to the following validation criteria:
* **Unedited Cargo-Opening Video**: You must record a single, continuous, unedited video of the outer parcel package being opened. 
* **Details Required in Video**: The video must clearly show the shipping label, the tracking ID bar code, all seals intact before opening, and the immediate extraction and inspection of the defective garment showing the specific flaw.
* **Submission Deadline**: The video documentation must be submitted to our wholesale liaison team at `mdshariq8176@gmail.com` within **48 hours** of delivery. Claims submitted without this unedited video or past the 48-hour window will be summarily rejected.

### 3. Dynamic RTO (Return to Origin) Routing
* In the event of a verified defect claim, our system will issue an automated Return to Origin (RTO) shipping label.
* The 3PL logistics routing is pre-configured to return the parcel directly to the manufacturer's main design facility:
  > **Safa Kurtilab (RTO Division)**
  > Vill-Hareknagar Mollabari, P.O. Hareknagar, P.S. Beldanga, District: Murshidabad, West Bengal - 742133
* Any shipments routed to alternate offices or warehouses without our pre-approved logistics tracking barcodes will not be accepted.

### 4. Credit Notes & Replacements
* Approved defect claims will be resolved via:
  1. An immediate replacement shipment of the identical variant/size set (subject to fabric availability).
  2. A wholesale store credit note valid for **180 days** toward future B2B purchases.
* Credit card or bank gateway refunds are not issued for bulk wholesale transactions except when replacements are entirely out of production.
