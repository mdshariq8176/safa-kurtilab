# Shipping & Delivery Policy

At **Safa Kurtilab**, we strive to deliver your designer clothing packages safely, securely, and as quickly as possible. We partner with India's leading B2B and retail logistics couriers (including Delhivery, BlueDart, and DTDC) to manage standard shipments.

---

### 1. Processing and Dispatch Timeline
* All standard catalog orders are processed, packed, and dispatched from our design studio warehouse within **24 to 48 hours** of order confirmation.
* Orders placed on Sundays or public holidays will be dispatched on the next working business day.

### 2. Delivery Timeframes
The delivery timeline depends on the destination address and pincode region:
* **Metro Cities (Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai, Bengaluru)**: Delivered within **3 to 5 business days** from the dispatch date.
* **Rest of India (Tier 2/3 cities and remote regions)**: Delivered within **5 to 7 business days** from the dispatch date.
* Please note that transit delays due to public blockages, severe weather, or courier operation backlogs are outside our control, though we work closely with courier support teams to resolve issues.

### 3. Order Tracking Alerts
* As soon as your order status changes to `SHIPPED` in our database, we automatically dispatch an SMS, email, and WhatsApp notification containing your **3PL Courier Tracking ID** and tracking URL link.
* You can track the progress of your shipment in real-time or schedule delivery instructions directly with the assigned courier partner.

### 4. Shipping Fees
* Standard shipping fees are calculated dynamically at checkout based on the total order weight and destination pin code.
* Bulk wholesale orders may receive custom shipping pricing depending on corporate logistics agreements.
