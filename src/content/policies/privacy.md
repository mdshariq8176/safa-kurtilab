# Privacy Policy

At **Safa Kurtilab**, accessible from www.safakurtilab.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Safa Kurtilab and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

---

### 1. Data Collection & Consent
By using our storefront or submitting billing details, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. We collect personal identifiers when you initiate checkout or create an account, including:
* Customer Name and Email Address
* Shipping and Billing Street Address, City, State, and Pincode
* Contact Phone Number (used exclusively for courier alerts, dispatch confirmations, and B2B communication)
* Corporate Details (optional Company Name and GSTIN) for commercial tax invoicing.

### 2. How We Use and Protect Your Data
We utilize high-grade database platforms to store and protect customer information:
* All database transactions, including order snapshots, user authentication details, and invoices, are stored securely inside our encrypted **Supabase PostgreSQL** cloud servers hosted in the Mumbai (`ap-south-1`) region.
* We enforce strict access control limits. Your customer data is **never** sold, rented, or distributed to third-party marketing services or commercial data brokers.

### 3. Log Files & Third-Party Service Providers
Safa Kurtilab follows standard procedures for utilizing log files and cloud server traces (e.g. Vercel logs) to monitor performance and debug database connection errors.

For fulfillment, we share limited customer detail arrays with authorized logistics partners:
* **Logistics & Courier Delivery**: Shipping details (Name, Address, Phone, Pincode) are shared with our 3PL logistics provider (**Shiprocket/Delhivery/BlueDart**) solely for packaging, routing, courier assignment, tracking generation, and parcel delivery.
* **Payment Processing**: Sensitive transaction data is securely processed via accredited gateway links (such as Razorpay/Cashfree) over tokenized HTTPS sockets. Safa Kurtilab does not store card numbers or banking passwords on our local database.

### 4. GDPR & CCPA Data Protection Rights
You have the right to request access to your records, correction of inaccuracies, or complete deletion of your customer profile from our Supabase tables. To invoke these rights, please contact our support team at concierge@safakurtilab.com.
